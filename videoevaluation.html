    The main method that we used for evaluating our prototype is user testing by conducting interviews.
    On the 26th of October, during our lab session, we were conducting interviews with some of our classmates and technical assistants; so that we can get some feedback from real users. Our aim was to:

    • Find out how interactive our current prototype is.
    • Ask users for their opinions as to how to make our product easy-to-use.
    • Ask users if there are any additional features that they would like to see in our product.
    • Find out their overall impression of the product.

    The feedback that we received from our peers and our TAs has been extremely helpful. As we were trying to get as much feedback as possible, we asked the users to think aloud, and we refrained from interrupting them. We can use the feedback to detect the potential usability problems. In the evaluation of the outcome page we will discuss the feedback that we gathered from the users and how we are going to try to fix them.
